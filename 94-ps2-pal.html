94. PS2 Pal

AI Physics Tutor

1. Startup Name

PS2 Pal

2. Founder Contact

Gregory Kestin

Harvard Physics Lecturer

Science educator and AI learning researcher

3. Product Overview

PS2 Pal is an AI tutor helping students learn physics through personalized guidance. The tool uses AI to guide students through physics problem-solving with hints and Socratic questioning rather than providing direct answers, developing deeper conceptual understanding.

4. Funding Stage

Research/Pilot — Being developed and tested in Harvard physics courses.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through Harvard AI in education research and physics teaching innovation coverage.

Strengths

  • Pedagogy-first: Designed by experienced educator with learning science focus.
  • STEM demand: Physics tutoring in high demand with limited quality options.

Weaknesses

  • Research stage: Not yet commercialized as standalone product.
  • Subject-specific: Physics focus limits initial market size.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. What learning outcomes have students achieved using PS2 Pal?
  2. How does the AI decide when to give hints vs. let students struggle?
  3. Are you planning to commercialize beyond Harvard courses?
  4. Can the approach extend to other STEM subjects?
  5. How do you ensure the AI doesn't make physics errors?

3. Product Overview

Tender Food uses proprietary nanofiber technology originally developed for tissue engineering to create plant-based meat with dramatically more realistic texture than existing products. The technology precisely controls fiber alignment to mimic the structure of animal muscle, addressing the texture gap that limits plant-based meat adoption. This Harvard-developed approach brings bioengineering expertise to the alternative protein market.

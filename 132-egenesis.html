3. Product Overview

eGenesis uses CRISPR gene editing to develop pig organs suitable for human transplantation (xenotransplantation). By making dozens of genetic modifications to pig cells, eGenesis removes pig viruses and modifies immune-related genes to prevent rejection. The company has achieved historic milestones including the first successful pig kidney transplants into living human patients, addressing the critical organ shortage crisis affecting 100,000+ patients on transplant waiting lists.

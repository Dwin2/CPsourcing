76. CiteRight

Legal Citation Collaboration Tool

Note: This company was sourced from HAE Accelerator 2023. Per assignment guidelines, HAE sources are typically excluded but included here for reference.

CiteRight

2. Founder Contact

Building legal tech tools

3. Product Overview

CiteRight is an essential litigation collaboration tool for lawyers and legal teams. The platform streamlines the process of managing legal citations, document collaboration, and brief preparation, helping legal teams work more efficiently on complex litigation matters.

4. Funding Stage

Seed — HAE Accelerator 2023 graduate.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through HAE Accelerator 2023 cohort announcements.

Strengths

  • Pain point clarity: Legal citation management is tedious and error-prone.
  • High-value customers: Law firms have significant budgets for productivity tools.

Weaknesses

  • Conservative buyers: Law firms slow to adopt new technology.
  • Competition: Established legal tech players like Thomson Reuters and LexisNexis.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. How many law firms are currently using your platform?
  2. What is the average time savings per brief or filing?
  3. How do you integrate with existing legal research tools?
  4. What is your pricing model — per seat, per firm, or per matter?
  5. How do you compete with citation features in Westlaw and Lexis?

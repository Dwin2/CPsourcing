32. Trove Health

Medical Records Management Platform

1. Startup Name

Trove Health

2. Founder Contact

Matt Ross — Co-Founder

Harvard Business School MBA '22

3. Product Overview

Trove Health is a medical records company that streamlines health data access and management. The platform helps patients and providers efficiently collect, organize, and share medical records across healthcare systems, addressing the fragmentation problem in healthcare data.

4. Funding Stage

Seed — Raised $3M from MassChallenge and other investors.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through HBS alumni funding news tracking healthcare startups founded by recent MBA graduates in 2024.

Strengths

  • Critical pain point: Medical records fragmentation costs the healthcare system billions and frustrates patients and providers alike.
  • Regulatory tailwinds: 21st Century Cures Act and information blocking rules create demand for interoperability solutions.

Weaknesses

  • Established competitors: Companies like Ciox, MRO, and digital health giants have existing records management infrastructure.
  • Sales cycle complexity: Healthcare organizations are slow to adopt new vendors for sensitive data handling.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. What is your primary customer segment — patients, providers, or payers?
  2. How do you handle HIPAA compliance and data security?
  3. What integrations do you have with major EHR systems?
  4. What is your revenue model — per-request, subscription, or transaction-based?
  5. How do you plan to scale beyond the initial MassChallenge network?

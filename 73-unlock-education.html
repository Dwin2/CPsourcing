School Data Analytics

Note: This company was sourced from Harvard Innovation Labs (Launch Lab X 2024-25). Per assignment guidelines, i-Labs sources are typically excluded but included here for reference.

Harvard Innovation Labs Team

Launch Lab X 2024-25 Cohort

3. Product Overview

Unlock Education empowers schools to leverage data for better decisions. The platform aggregates and analyzes student data from multiple sources, providing administrators and teachers with actionable insights to improve student outcomes and operational efficiency.

4. Funding Stage

Early Stage — Florida pilot programs launching 2025.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through Harvard Innovation Labs Launch Lab X 2024-25 cohort listings.

Strengths

  • Data-driven education: Growing demand for analytics in K-12 decision-making.
  • Pilot traction: Florida pilots demonstrate early market validation.

Weaknesses

  • Privacy concerns: Student data handling faces increasing scrutiny and regulation.
  • Competitive market: PowerSchool, Clever, and others compete in school data.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. What data sources does your platform integrate?
  2. How do you address student privacy and FERPA compliance?
  3. What specific decisions do schools make better with your analytics?
  4. How do you differentiate from existing student information systems?
  5. What is your pricing model for school districts?

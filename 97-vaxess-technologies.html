97. Vaxess Technologies

Thermostable Vaccine Delivery

Note: This company was sourced from President's Innovation Challenge. Per assignment guidelines, i-Labs sources are typically excluded but included here for reference.

1. Startup Name

Vaxess Technologies

2. Founder Contact

Harvard PIC Winner

Harvard Innovation Labs / President's Innovation Challenge

Founded on Wyss Institute silk protein technology

3. Product Overview

Vaxess Technologies develops thermostable vaccine delivery using silk protein technology. The MIMIX platform uses silk-based patches to deliver vaccines without refrigeration, potentially revolutionizing vaccine distribution in developing countries where cold chain logistics are challenging.

4. Funding Stage

Series A — Raised $8M+ to advance vaccine patch development.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through President's Innovation Challenge winners and vaccine innovation coverage.

Strengths

  • Global health impact: Cold chain elimination could save millions of vaccine doses.
  • Novel technology: Silk protein platform has unique stability properties.

Weaknesses

  • Clinical validation: Must prove patch delivery is as effective as injection.
  • Pharma partnerships: Need vaccine manufacturers to adopt platform.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. What vaccines have you validated with your silk protein platform?
  2. What is the shelf life without refrigeration?
  3. How do you partner with vaccine manufacturers?
  4. What is your regulatory pathway for vaccine delivery devices?
  5. Which global health organizations are you working with?

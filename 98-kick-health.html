98. Kick Health

Performance Anxiety Telemedicine

1. Startup Name

Kick Health

2. Founder Contact

Harvard/Stanford/Yale Partners

Harvard Psychiatry partners

Medical leadership from top academic institutions

3. Product Overview

Kick Health provides telemedicine for performance anxiety and sleep issues. The platform connects patients with psychiatrists who can prescribe beta-blockers and other medications for situational anxiety, helping professionals manage performance-related stress.

4. Funding Stage

Seed Stage — Building telehealth platform for underserved mental health conditions.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through mental health telemedicine coverage and telehealth innovation research.

Strengths

  • Underserved niche: Performance anxiety undertreated despite high prevalence.
  • Simple treatment: Beta-blockers are safe, effective, and well-understood.

Weaknesses

  • Narrow focus: Performance anxiety is specific use case.
  • Telehealth competition: Many platforms now offer mental health prescriptions.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. How many patients have you treated through the platform?
  2. What is your clinical protocol for prescribing beta-blockers?
  3. How do you differentiate from broader telehealth platforms?
  4. Are you expanding to other mental health conditions?
  5. What is your customer acquisition strategy?

3. Product Overview

Metalmark develops butterfly-wing inspired nano-coatings for air purification. The technology uses nanostructured materials that mimic butterfly wing surfaces to catalytically destroy airborne pathogens, VOCs, and pollutants at room temperature without generating harmful byproducts. The coatings can be applied to HVAC systems and air filters for improved indoor air quality.

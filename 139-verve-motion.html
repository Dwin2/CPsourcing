3. Product Overview

Verve Motion develops the SafeLift exosuit, a wearable powered device that reduces back strain by 30-40% for workers performing repetitive lifting and bending tasks. Unlike rigid exoskeletons, the soft exosuit design from the Walsh lab at the Wyss Institute is lightweight, comfortable for all-day wear, and doesn't restrict natural movement. The system is deployed in warehouses, manufacturing, and logistics to reduce workplace injuries and improve worker productivity.

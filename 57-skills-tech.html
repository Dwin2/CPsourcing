57. Skills.tech

AI Medical Education Platform

1. Startup Name

Skills.tech

2. Founder Contact

HMS Team

Harvard Medical School

President's Innovation Challenge 2024 Finalist

3. Product Overview

Skills.tech creates AI-crafted learning experiences for medical education and corporate training. The platform uses artificial intelligence to personalize educational content, simulate clinical scenarios, and assess competency for healthcare professionals and corporate learners.

4. Funding Stage

Early Stage — President's Innovation Challenge 2024 Finalist, developing MVP.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through Harvard President's Innovation Challenge 2024 finalist announcements.

Strengths

  • AI education trend: Healthcare education increasingly adopting AI-powered simulation.
  • Harvard credibility: HMS backing provides access to medical education networks.

Weaknesses

  • Competitive market: Medical education platforms like Osmosis and Lecturio well-established.
  • Sales cycle: Medical schools have long procurement cycles.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. What specific medical skills does your platform teach?
  2. How does your AI personalize learning paths for individual students?
  3. What clinical outcomes data do you have from pilot programs?
  4. Are you targeting medical schools, residency programs, or continuing education?
  5. How do you ensure AI-generated content meets medical accuracy standards?

37. Beaver Health

Cognitive Health for Older Adults

1. Startup Name

Beaver Health

2. Founder Contact

Harvard PIC Team

Harvard College / Harvard Innovation Labs

3. Product Overview

Beaver Health engages older loved ones in stimulating, culturally responsive activities to boost cognition and quality of life. The platform addresses cognitive decline prevention through personalized, engaging content tailored to diverse cultural backgrounds and individual preferences.

4. Funding Stage

Pre-Seed — Won $75,000 President's Innovation Challenge 2024 award. Also received two National Institutes of Health grants. Featured by National Institute on Aging at What's Next Longevity Venture Summit.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through Harvard President's Innovation Challenge 2024 winners announcement and Harvard Innovation Labs milestone tracking.

Strengths

  • Growing market: Aging population and increased focus on cognitive health creates massive demand.
  • NIH validation: Federal grant funding demonstrates scientific credibility and de-risks early research.

Weaknesses

  • Early stage: Very early in development with limited traction data.
  • Consumer behavior challenge: Engaging older adults with digital health tools requires careful UX design.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. What clinical evidence supports the efficacy of your cognitive activities?
  2. How do you acquire and engage users — through families, senior centers, or healthcare providers?
  3. What is your revenue model — B2C subscription, B2B licensing, or care facility partnerships?
  4. How do you handle cultural customization across different ethnic and linguistic communities?
  5. What are your plans for the NIH grant milestones and next funding round?

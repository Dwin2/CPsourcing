3. Product Overview

Speetar is an AI-powered digital health platform revolutionizing healthcare delivery in conflict-affected and low-resource settings. The platform connects patients with medical providers worldwide, serving millions in crisis regions.

4. Funding Stage

Growth Stage — Echoing Green Fellowship recipient. Has served millions of patients.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through Echoing Green Fellows directory highlighting Harvard-affiliated social entrepreneurs in healthcare.

Strengths

Weaknesses

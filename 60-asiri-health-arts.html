60. ASIRI Health Arts

Expressive Arts Mental Health

1. Startup Name

ASIRI Health Arts

2. Founder Contact

HSPH Fellow

Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health

Harvard Innovation Labs participant

3. Product Overview

ASIRI Health Arts develops an expressive art framework advocating for mental health wellness. The platform uses creative arts therapy approaches to help individuals process emotions, reduce stress, and improve mental well-being through guided artistic expression.

4. Funding Stage

Early Stage — Developing through Harvard Innovation Labs support.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through Harvard Innovation Labs portfolio and mental health innovation coverage.

Strengths

  • Growing mental health awareness: Alternative therapy approaches gaining acceptance.
  • Low barrier to entry: Art therapy can be delivered digitally at scale.

Weaknesses

  • Efficacy evidence: Art therapy has less clinical validation than traditional therapy.
  • Reimbursement: Insurance rarely covers art therapy programs.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. What specific mental health outcomes does your program target?
  2. How do you deliver the art therapy experience — app, video, or in-person?
  3. What clinical evidence do you have for program effectiveness?
  4. Is your model B2C or B2B (employer wellness, health systems)?
  5. How do you train or certify facilitators for your programs?

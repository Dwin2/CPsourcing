55. Rejuvenate Bio

Anti-Aging Gene Therapy

1. Startup Name

Rejuvenate Bio

2. Founder Contact

Wyss Institute Team

Wyss Institute for Biologically Inspired Engineering at Harvard

Co-founded with Dr. George Church, Harvard genetics pioneer

3. Product Overview

Rejuvenate Bio develops gene therapy to treat age-related conditions, starting with companion animals like dogs. The company uses gene therapy to deliver combinations of longevity-associated genes, aiming to extend healthy lifespan and treat age-related diseases in pets before translating to humans.

4. Funding Stage

Series A — Raised $10M+ to advance veterinary gene therapy programs.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through Wyss Institute spinout company portfolio and longevity research coverage.

Strengths

  • Pet market entry: Veterinary pathway allows faster validation with lower regulatory burden.
  • Star power: George Church association brings credibility and attention.

Weaknesses

  • Unproven science: Longevity gene therapy remains highly experimental.
  • Ethical concerns: Human anti-aging interventions face significant public scrutiny.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. What results have you seen in canine trials?
  2. Which specific age-related conditions are you targeting first?
  3. What is your timeline for human applications?
  4. How do you price gene therapy for pet owners?
  5. What safety data do you have on multi-gene therapy approaches?

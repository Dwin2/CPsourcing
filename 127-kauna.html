3. Product Overview

Kauna focuses on population health management for chronic kidney disease (CKD), one of the most costly and prevalent chronic conditions affecting millions of Americans. The platform uses data analytics and care coordination tools to identify at-risk patients, optimize treatment pathways, and slow disease progression to reduce dialysis dependency and improve patient outcomes.

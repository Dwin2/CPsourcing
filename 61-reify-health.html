61. Reify Health

Clinical Trial Management Platform

1. Startup Name

Reify Health

2. Founder Contact

Rock Health Boston Partners

Harvard Medical School affiliated partners

Rock Health Boston accelerator cohort

3. Product Overview

Reify Health is a clinical trial management platform accelerating drug development. The software helps pharmaceutical companies and research sites recruit patients, manage trial operations, and reduce the time and cost of bringing new drugs to market.

4. Funding Stage

Series D — Raised $220M+ from investors including Andreessen Horowitz.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through Rock Health Boston portfolio and clinical trial innovation coverage.

Strengths

  • Massive market: Clinical trial market exceeds $50B annually with major inefficiencies.
  • Strong traction: Series D funding indicates significant customer adoption.

Weaknesses

  • Late-stage valuation: Significant funding may limit upside for new investors.
  • Competitive market: Medidata, Veeva, and others compete in clinical trial software.

  1. How many clinical trials are currently running on your platform?
  2. What is your average reduction in patient recruitment time?
  3. How do you differentiate from Medidata and Veeva?
  4. What therapeutic areas have the highest platform adoption?

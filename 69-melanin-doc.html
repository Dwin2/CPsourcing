69. Melanin Doc

Minority Medical Student Support

1. Startup Name

Melanin Doc

2. Founder Contact

HBS Team

Harvard Business School

President's Innovation Challenge 2023 Finalist

3. Product Overview

Melanin Doc provides scholarships and mentorship for minority students pursuing medicine. The organization works to increase representation in healthcare by supporting underrepresented students through their medical education journey with financial assistance and professional guidance.

4. Funding Stage

Early Stage — President's Innovation Challenge 2023 Finalist.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through Harvard President's Innovation Challenge 2023 finalist announcements.

Strengths

  • Critical need: Only 5% of physicians are Black, creating healthcare disparities.
  • High impact: Diversifying physician workforce improves care for minority patients.

Weaknesses

  • Fundraising dependent: Scholarship programs require constant fundraising.
  • Long-term results: Medical training takes 7-11 years to see physician outcomes.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. How many scholarships have you awarded to date?
  2. What is the average scholarship amount per recipient?
  3. How do you select scholarship recipients?
  4. What mentorship programs do you provide beyond financial support?
  5. How do you track long-term outcomes for scholarship recipients?

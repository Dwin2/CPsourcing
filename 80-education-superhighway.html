80. Education SuperHighway

School Internet Connectivity

1. Startup Name

Education SuperHighway

2. Founder Contact

Evan Marwell

Harvard Kennedy School

Tech entrepreneur turned education advocate

3. Product Overview

Education SuperHighway closed the digital divide by connecting schools to high-speed internet. The organization worked with school districts and state governments to upgrade network infrastructure, successfully achieving its goal of connecting 99% of American classrooms to high-speed broadband.

4. Funding Stage

Mission Complete — Nonprofit achieved its goal and sunset in 2022.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through Harvard SICI social enterprise network and education infrastructure coverage.

Strengths

  • Mission accomplished: Rare example of nonprofit achieving and ending at goal.
  • Massive impact: Connected 47M students to high-speed internet.

Weaknesses

  • Historical case study: Organization no longer operating.
  • Not investable: Completed mission, no ongoing entity.

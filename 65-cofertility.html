65. Cofertility

Egg Freezing and Donation Platform

1. Startup Name

Cofertility

2. Founder Contact

Halle Tecco

Harvard Business School

Founder of Rock Health, serial femtech entrepreneur

3. Product Overview

Cofertility is an egg freezing and donation platform making fertility care accessible. The company offers a unique model where women can freeze their eggs for free if they donate half to intended parents, creating a win-win for women seeking to preserve fertility and families building through donation.

4. Funding Stage

Seed — Raised $5M+ to build egg freezing and donation marketplace.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through Rock Health founder portfolio and women's health coverage.

Strengths

  • Innovative model: Free egg freezing through donation creates unique value proposition.
  • Market timing: Egg freezing demand growing as women delay childbearing.

Weaknesses

  • Ethical complexity: Egg donation models face scrutiny and regulatory oversight.
  • Two-sided marketplace: Must balance donor supply with recipient demand.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. How many women have enrolled in your egg freezing program?
  2. How do you screen and match donors with intended parents?
  3. What is the conversion rate from egg freezing interest to donation commitment?
  4. How do you handle the ethical considerations of egg donation?
  5. What are your expansion plans beyond current markets?

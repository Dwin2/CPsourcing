63. Africa Healthcare Network

Dialysis Care in Africa

1. Startup Name

Africa Healthcare Network

2. Founder Contact

Nikhil Pereira-Kamath

Harvard Business School '23

Building healthcare infrastructure in Sub-Saharan Africa

3. Product Overview

Africa Healthcare Network operates a dialysis chain expanding kidney care access in Sub-Saharan Africa. The company builds and operates dialysis centers to serve the millions of Africans with kidney disease who currently lack access to life-saving treatment.

4. Funding Stage

Growth Stage — Raised $20M to expand dialysis center network across Africa.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through HBS alumni funding news and global health infrastructure coverage.

Strengths

  • Massive unmet need: Only 5% of Africans needing dialysis currently have access.
  • First-mover advantage: Limited competition in African dialysis market.

Weaknesses

  • Infrastructure challenges: Operating medical equipment in African markets is complex.
  • Payment models: Limited insurance and government coverage in many countries.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. How many dialysis centers are you currently operating?
  2. What countries are you active in and what is your expansion plan?
  3. How do patients pay — insurance, government, out-of-pocket?
  4. How do you ensure quality and safety in resource-limited settings?
  5. What is your cost per dialysis session compared to global averages?

42. Hometeam

Caregiver Support Platform

1. Startup Name

Hometeam

2. Founder Contact

HBS Team — Harvard Business School

3. Product Overview

Hometeam enables family and paid caregivers to deliver the best care possible, as efficiently as possible. The platform provides tools for care coordination, task management, and communication among care teams.

4. Funding Stage

Early Stage — PIC 2024 Finalist

5. How I Found the Company

President's Innovation Challenge 2024 finalist announcements.

Strengths

  • Growing caregiver crisis: 53M Americans serve as unpaid caregivers, spending $470B+ annually.
  • B2B2C potential: Employers and insurers increasingly offer caregiver benefits.

Weaknesses

  • Fragmented market: Many point solutions exist for different caregiver needs.
  • Consumer adoption: Stressed caregivers may resist adding new tools.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. Who is your primary customer — family caregivers, paid caregivers, or care agencies?
  2. What is your revenue model?
  3. How do you integrate with healthcare providers and insurers?
  4. What metrics show improved caregiver outcomes?
  5. How do you differentiate from Care.com, Caring Village, and other platforms?

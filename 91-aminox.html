91. AminoX

Non-Standard Amino Acid Therapeutics

1. Startup Name

AminoX

2. Founder Contact

Wyss Institute Team

Wyss Institute for Biologically Inspired Engineering at Harvard

Backed by Northpond Ventures

3. Product Overview

AminoX develops a novel approach for incorporating non-standard amino acids into protein drugs. The platform expands the chemical diversity of therapeutic proteins, enabling the development of biologics with improved efficacy, stability, and safety profiles.

4. Funding Stage

Early Stage — Developing technology with Wyss Institute and Northpond support.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through Wyss Institute and Northpond Ventures portfolio coverage.

Strengths

  • Platform technology: Could improve multiple therapeutic proteins.
  • Strong backers: Wyss and Northpond provide research and capital support.

Weaknesses

  • Early stage: Technology still in development phase.
  • Complex manufacturing: Non-standard amino acids add production complexity.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. What therapeutic proteins are you initially targeting?
  2. How do you manufacture proteins with non-standard amino acids at scale?
  3. What improvements in efficacy or stability have you demonstrated?
  4. Are you developing internal programs or partnering with pharma?
  5. What is your regulatory pathway for modified biologics?

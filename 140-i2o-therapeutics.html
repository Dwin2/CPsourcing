3. Product Overview

i2O Therapeutics develops oral formulations of insulin and other injectable biologics using proprietary ionic liquid technology. The platform enables proteins like insulin to survive the harsh gastrointestinal environment and be absorbed into the bloodstream, potentially replacing daily injections with oral pills. For the 450+ million diabetics worldwide who require insulin injections, an oral alternative would be transformative for quality of life and adherence.

Pre-Seed — Part of Harvard Alumni Entrepreneurs Accelerator 2024 cohort (equity-free). Exact funding not publicly disclosed.

5. How I Found the Company

I discovered Platform through the Harvard Alumni Entrepreneurs Accelerator 2024 cohort announcement. While Construction Tech is not strictly Digital Health or Education, the company addresses a massive industry ($12T+ globally) that is notoriously slow to adopt technology. The HAE selection suggests the team and approach met rigorous evaluation criteria.

Strengths

  • Massive market with technology adoption lag: The global construction industry exceeds $12 trillion annually but has had minimal productivity growth for decades. Solutions that genuinely improve efficiency could capture significant value.
  • HAE selection validation: The Harvard Alumni Entrepreneurs Accelerator selects from a competitive pool, suggesting the Platform Method™ approach passed initial due diligence.

Weaknesses

  • Different sector focus: Platform is a Construction Tech company, not in the Digital Health or Education verticals.
  • Limited public information: Details about the Platform Method™ and the company's specific value proposition are not readily available, making assessment difficult without direct engagement.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. What specifically is the Platform Method™ — is it a software platform, a construction methodology, or a combination?
  2. What construction segments are you targeting — residential, commercial, infrastructure, or a specific vertical?
  3. What measurable results (time savings, cost reduction, quality improvement) have you demonstrated?
  4. How do you go to market — direct to general contractors, through developers, or via technology licensing?
  5. What is your competitive differentiation versus other construction tech solutions (Procore, PlanGrid, etc.)?

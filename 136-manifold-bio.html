3. Product Overview

Manifold Bio uses proprietary protein barcoding technology to enable testing of hundreds of drug candidates simultaneously in a single animal study. By attaching unique molecular barcodes to protein therapeutics, Manifold can track the behavior of many candidates in parallel, dramatically accelerating the drug discovery process while reducing animal use and costs. This multiplexed approach can compress years of traditional sequential testing into months.

Brain Disorder Therapeutics

1. Startup Name

3. Product Overview

MapLight Therapeutics develops therapeutics for brain disorders and neurological diseases. The company focuses on novel mechanisms to treat conditions like schizophrenia and other CNS disorders where current treatments have significant limitations.

4. Funding Stage

Series C — Raised $225M to advance clinical development programs.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through HBS alumni funding news and neuroscience therapeutics coverage.

Strengths

  • Huge unmet need: CNS disorders affect hundreds of millions with limited treatment options.
  • Experienced team: Serial entrepreneurs with pharma development expertise.

Weaknesses

  • CNS drug development risk: Brain disorder drugs have historically high failure rates.
  • Long timelines: CNS clinical trials often take 10+ years to complete.

77. Zette

Pay-Per-Article Publishing Access

Note: This company was sourced from HAE Accelerator 2023. Per assignment guidelines, HAE sources are typically excluded but included here for reference.

1. Startup Name

Zette

2. Founder Contact

HAE 2023 Cohort

Harvard Alumni Entrepreneurs Accelerator 2023

Disrupting publishing access models

3. Product Overview

Zette provides pay-per-article access to paywalled publications with revenue sharing. The platform allows readers to purchase individual articles from premium publications without full subscriptions, while sharing revenue fairly with publishers and journalists.

4. Funding Stage

Seed — HAE Accelerator 2023 graduate.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through HAE Accelerator 2023 cohort announcements.

Strengths

  • Consumer demand: Many readers want occasional access without full subscriptions.
  • Publisher interest: New revenue stream for struggling journalism industry.

Weaknesses

  • Publisher partnerships: Difficult to get major publications to participate.
  • Micropayment challenges: Previous pay-per-article models have struggled.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. How many publications are currently on your platform?
  2. What is the average article price and revenue split with publishers?
  3. How do you drive reader discovery and purchase intent?
  4. What is your user retention and repeat purchase rate?
  5. How do you compete with browser extensions that bypass paywalls?

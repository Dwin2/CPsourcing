41. PeriPeach

Pelvic Floor Protection Device

1. Startup Name

PeriPeach

2. Founder Contact

HMS Team — Harvard Medical School

3. Product Overview

PeriPeach is designing a medical device that protects the pelvic floor during childbirth, addressing the common but often overlooked issue of perineal trauma that affects millions of women annually.

4. Funding Stage

Early Stage — PIC Finalist

5. How I Found the Company

President's Innovation Challenge 2023 health track finalist announcements.

Strengths

  • Clear clinical need: Up to 85% of women experience perineal trauma during vaginal delivery.
  • Medical device pathway: Clear FDA regulatory path for birthing devices.

Weaknesses

  • Hospital adoption: L&D units are conservative about new interventions.
  • Reimbursement uncertainty: New devices need coverage to achieve adoption.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. What clinical trials have you completed or planned?
  2. What is your FDA regulatory strategy?
  3. How do you plan to achieve hospital adoption?
  4. What is your manufacturing and cost structure?
  5. Who are your clinical advisors in obstetrics?

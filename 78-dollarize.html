78. Dollarize

Currency Protection Platform

Note: This company was sourced from HAE Accelerator 2023. Per assignment guidelines, HAE sources are typically excluded but included here for reference.

1. Startup Name

Dollarize

2. Founder Contact

HAE 2023 Cohort

Harvard Alumni Entrepreneurs Accelerator 2023

Building fintech for emerging markets

3. Product Overview

Dollarize is a financial access platform protecting foreign nationals from currency devaluation. The platform allows people in countries with unstable currencies to hold and transact in US dollars, protecting their savings from inflation and devaluation.

4. Funding Stage

Seed — HAE Accelerator 2023 graduate.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through HAE Accelerator 2023 cohort announcements.

Strengths

  • Urgent need: Millions in Latin America, Africa, and elsewhere face currency instability.
  • Mobile-first: Smartphone penetration enables fintech adoption in emerging markets.

Weaknesses

  • Regulatory complexity: Cross-border dollar access faces significant regulatory hurdles.
  • Competition: Crypto, stablecoins, and traditional remittance compete for same customers.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. What countries are you currently operating in?
  2. How do you comply with local currency control regulations?
  3. What is your fee structure compared to traditional forex?
  4. How do you acquire customers in target markets?
  5. Are you using stablecoins or traditional banking rails?

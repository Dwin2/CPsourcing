Digital Health

36. Komodo Health

Healthcare Intelligence Platform

1. Startup Name

Komodo Health

2. Founder Contact

Arif Nathoo, MD MPA — Co-Founder & CEO

Harvard Medical School MD, Harvard Kennedy School MPA

3. Product Overview

Komodo Health is a healthcare intelligence company that maps patient journeys across the entire healthcare system. Using the Healthcare Map™ — the industry's largest patient-level dataset — they enable life sciences companies, payers, and providers to make data-driven decisions about drug development, commercialization, and care delivery.

4. Funding Stage

Late Stage — Raised over $600M from investors including ICONIQ Growth, Tiger Global, and HealthQuest Capital. Valued at approximately $3.3B.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered as a prominent Harvard HMS/HKS alumni success story, frequently featured in healthcare intelligence and life sciences technology coverage.

Strengths

  • Unmatched data asset: Healthcare Map covers 330M+ patient journeys, creating significant barriers to entry.
  • Diversified revenue: Serves pharma, payers, and providers with different use cases and buying cycles.

Weaknesses

  • Late-stage valuation: $3.3B valuation may limit upside for new investors in current market.
  • Regulatory risk: Healthcare data privacy regulations could impact data collection and usage.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. How do you maintain data freshness and accuracy across your Healthcare Map?
  2. What is your revenue breakdown between pharma, payer, and provider segments?
  3. How do you navigate HIPAA and state privacy regulations for patient-level data?
  4. What is your competitive moat versus IQVIA, Veeva, and other healthcare data companies?
  5. What are your IPO plans given the current market conditions?

3. Product Overview

MonitAir provides remote patient monitoring solutions specifically designed for sleep conditions including sleep apnea, insomnia, and related disorders. The platform combines wearable sensors with AI-powered analytics to enable continuous monitoring of sleep quality, CPAP adherence, and treatment effectiveness, allowing healthcare providers to manage patients remotely and intervene proactively.

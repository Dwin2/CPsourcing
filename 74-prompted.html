3. Product Overview

PromptED supports educators in AI-driven learning design. The platform helps teachers create effective AI-enhanced lessons, assignments, and assessments, enabling them to harness AI tools while maintaining pedagogical rigor and learning objectives.

4. Funding Stage

Early Stage — Developing through Harvard Innovation Labs support.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through Harvard Innovation Labs Launch Lab X 2024-25 cohort listings.

Strengths

  • Timely need: Teachers urgently need guidance on incorporating AI in education.
  • Teacher-centric: Focusing on educators rather than students addresses key gap.

Weaknesses

  • Rapidly changing field: AI in education evolving so fast that tools may become outdated.
  • Teacher time: Educators have limited time for professional development.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. What specific AI tools does your platform help teachers implement?
  2. How do you keep curriculum current as AI capabilities evolve?
  3. Is your model self-service or do you provide professional development?
  4. What subject areas or grade levels are you initially targeting?
  5. How do you measure improvement in teaching effectiveness?

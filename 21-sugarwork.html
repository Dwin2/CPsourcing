3. Product Overview

Sugarwork is an enterprise SaaS platform that uses generative AI to help companies capture tacit knowledge — the undocumented expertise, relationships, and institutional memory that walks out the door when employees retire or leave. The platform enables knowledge transfer from aging workforces to newer employees.

3. Product Overview

SeeAir uses AI and smartphone technology to enable rapid home energy assessments for decarbonization. Homeowners can conduct comprehensive assessments using just their smartphone camera, which SeeAir's AI analyzes to identify opportunities for energy efficiency upgrades, electrification, and carbon reduction. The platform streamlines the pathway to accessing MassSave and other utility incentive programs.

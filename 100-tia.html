3. Product Overview

Tia operates modern women's healthcare clinics combining virtual and in-person care. The company provides comprehensive women's health services including primary care, gynecology, and mental health in beautifully designed clinics, integrated with a digital platform for ongoing care.

4. Funding Stage

Series B — Raised $100M+ to expand clinic network nationally.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through women's health leaders coverage and healthcare clinic innovation research.

Strengths

  • Strong brand: Tia has built a beloved consumer brand in women's health.
  • Full-stack model: Owning clinics and technology enables integrated care.

Weaknesses

  • Capital intensive: Clinic expansion requires significant real estate investment.
  • Local scaling: Physical clinics must be built market by market.

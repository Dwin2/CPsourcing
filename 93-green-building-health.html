93. Green Building Health

Healthy Building Development Tool

1. Startup Name

Green Building Health

2. Founder Contact

Adele Houghton, DrPH

Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health '23

HealthLab Accelerators participant

3. Product Overview

Green Building Health provides a digital tool connecting developers and communities for healthy green buildings. The platform helps real estate developers assess and communicate the health benefits of sustainable building practices to community stakeholders.

4. Funding Stage

Early Stage — Developed through HealthLab Accelerators program.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through HealthLab Accelerators program and healthy buildings research.

Strengths

  • ESG trends: Real estate increasingly focused on health and sustainability.
  • Research-backed: Harvard public health expertise provides credibility.

Weaknesses

  • Early stage: Still developing product and go-to-market strategy.
  • Niche market: Health-focused development tools are specialized.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. What specific features does your tool provide to developers?
  2. How do you quantify health benefits of building practices?
  3. What types of developers are your target customers?
  4. How do you integrate with existing building certification systems like WELL?
  5. What is your revenue model — subscription, per-project, or consulting?

89. Ultivue

DNA-PAINT Imaging Technology

1. Startup Name

Ultivue

2. Founder Contact

Wyss Institute Team

Wyss Institute for Biologically Inspired Engineering at Harvard

Founded on DNA-PAINT super-resolution imaging technology

3. Product Overview

Ultivue develops innovative imaging solutions using DNA-PAINT technology. The platform enables high-resolution multiplexed tissue imaging for cancer research and diagnostics, allowing researchers to visualize multiple biomarkers simultaneously with unprecedented clarity.

4. Funding Stage

Series B — Raised significant venture funding for product development and commercialization.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through Wyss Institute spinout company portfolio and precision medicine coverage.

Strengths

  • Technology leadership: DNA-PAINT offers unique advantages for multiplexed imaging.
  • Growing market: Spatial biology market projected to exceed $1B by 2027.

Weaknesses

  • Competitive field: Akoya, Nanostring, and others compete in spatial biology.
  • Academic adoption: Research tools have long adoption cycles.

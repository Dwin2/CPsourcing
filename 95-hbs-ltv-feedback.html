95. HBS LTV Feedback

Custom GPT for Business Education

1. Startup Name

3. Product Overview

HBS LTV Feedback is a custom GPT providing feedback on student papers and startup ideas. The tool was developed by Professor Bussgang to give students immediate, personalized feedback on their venture concepts and written work in his entrepreneurship courses.

4. Funding Stage

Internal Tool — Currently used within HBS courses, not commercialized.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through HBS AI teaching innovation coverage and entrepreneurship education research.

Strengths

  • Immediate feedback: Scales personalized coaching for large classes.

Weaknesses

  • Internal tool: Not currently available as commercial product.
  • Course-specific: Designed for HBS curriculum and context.

3. Product Overview

GuardianSat addresses space debris management in Geostationary Earth Orbit (GEO), developing solutions to track, manage, and mitigate orbital debris that threatens critical satellite infrastructure.

4. Funding Stage

Pre-Seed — Winner of HAE Accelerator 2024 Demo Day. Advanced to compete at Startup World Cup for $1M investment opportunity. Part of Harvard Alumni Entrepreneurs Accelerator 2024 cohort (equity-free).

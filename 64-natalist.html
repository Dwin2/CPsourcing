64. Natalist

Fertility and Pregnancy Wellness

1. Startup Name

Natalist

2. Founder Contact

Halle Tecco

Harvard Business School

Founder of Rock Health, serial femtech entrepreneur

3. Product Overview

Natalist provides fertility and pregnancy wellness products for modern families. The company offers a curated line of ovulation tests, prenatal vitamins, pregnancy tests, and other fertility products designed to be more accessible and user-friendly than traditional options.

4. Funding Stage

Seed Stage — Building direct-to-consumer fertility brand.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through Rock Health founder portfolio and women's health coverage.

Strengths

  • Proven founder: Halle Tecco founded Rock Health and has deep femtech expertise.
  • Growing market: Fertility products market expanding as family planning age increases.

Weaknesses

  • Consumer brand building: D2C fertility requires significant marketing spend.
  • Competition: Modern Fertility (acquired by Ro), Ava, and others in market.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. What is your product lineup and which products drive most revenue?
  2. How do you acquire customers cost-effectively?
  3. Are you expanding into clinical services or telehealth?
  4. What is your retail distribution strategy?
  5. How do you differentiate from competitors like Clearblue and Modern Fertility?

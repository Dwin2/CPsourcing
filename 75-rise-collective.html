75. Rise Collective

Youth Mentorship Fellowship

1. Startup Name

Rise Collective

2. Founder Contact

Harvard Innovation Labs Team

Launch Lab X 2024-25 Cohort

Building youth career development programs

3. Product Overview

Rise Collective is a student fellowship connecting youth to mentors for career development. The program pairs high school and college students with professionals in their fields of interest, providing structured mentorship and career exploration opportunities.

4. Funding Stage

Early Stage — Fellowship program launched and operating.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through Harvard Innovation Labs Launch Lab X 2024-25 cohort listings.

Strengths

  • Social capital gap: Many students lack professional networks that privileged peers have.
  • Low-tech solution: Mentorship programs don't require expensive technology development.

Weaknesses

  • Scaling mentorship: Quality mentorship programs are difficult to scale.
  • Mentor recruitment: Finding and retaining quality mentors is ongoing challenge.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. How many student-mentor pairs have you facilitated?
  2. How do you recruit and vet mentors?
  3. What outcomes do you track for fellowship participants?
  4. Is this a nonprofit or do you have a revenue model?
  5. How do you ensure mentorship quality and consistency?

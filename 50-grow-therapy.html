50. Grow Therapy

Teletherapy Practice Enablement

1. Startup Name

Grow Therapy

2. Founder Contact

HMS-affiliated Founders

Leadership from Harvard Medical School, Stripe, and Blackstone backgrounds

3. Product Overview

Grow Therapy enables therapists to launch their own in-network teletherapy practices. The platform handles credentialing, billing, insurance contracting, and practice management, allowing therapists to focus on patient care.

4. Funding Stage

Series C — Raised $178M from TCV, SignalFire, and others at $1B+ valuation.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through mental health tech industry coverage and tracking Harvard-affiliated healthcare founders.

Strengths

  • Massive market: Mental health demand surged post-COVID with ongoing therapist shortages.
  • Insurance-first model: In-network positioning makes therapy more affordable and accessible.

Weaknesses

  • Competitive pressure: Headway, Alma, and others compete for therapist supply.
  • Margin compression: Insurance reimbursement rates limit pricing power.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. How many therapists are currently on your platform?
  2. What is your therapist retention rate and satisfaction score?
  3. How do you compete with Headway and Alma for therapist acquisition?
  4. What is your path to profitability given the high customer acquisition costs?
  5. How are you expanding beyond therapy to other mental health services?

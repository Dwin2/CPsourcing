72. Classy AI

Ethical Socratic Learning Companion

1. Startup Name

Classy AI

2. Founder Contact

Harvard Innovation Labs Team

Launch Lab X 2024-25 Cohort

Building ethical AI tutoring technology

3. Product Overview

Classy AI is an ethical Socratic learning companion teaching students how to think. The platform uses AI to guide students through problems with questions rather than answers, developing critical thinking skills while preventing academic dishonesty concerns associated with AI tools.

4. Funding Stage

Pre-Seed — MVP launched, building early user base.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through Harvard Innovation Labs Launch Lab X 2024-25 cohort listings.

Strengths

  • Ethical positioning: Socratic method addresses concerns about AI enabling cheating.
  • School-friendly: Schools more likely to adopt tools that teach thinking vs. provide answers.

Weaknesses

  • User preference: Students may prefer tools that give direct answers.
  • Differentiation: Other AI tutors also claim Socratic approaches.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. How do you ensure the AI guides without giving away answers?
  2. What subjects or grade levels are you targeting initially?
  3. How do schools verify students aren't using answer-giving AI instead?
  4. What learning outcome data do you have from early users?
  5. Are you selling to schools (B2B) or directly to students (B2C)?

47. Aledade

Primary Care ACO Network

1. Startup Name

Aledade

2. Founder Contact

Farzad Mostashari, MD MPH — Co-Founder & CEO

Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health MPH

Former National Coordinator for Health IT under Obama administration

3. Product Overview

Aledade is the nation's largest network of independent primary care practices organized into accountable care organizations (ACOs). The company helps practices succeed in value-based care, saving Medicare over $1 billion in 2024 while improving quality for 1M+ patients.

4. Funding Stage

Late Stage / Unicorn — Raised $500M+ from ICONIQ, Venrock, GV, and others. Valued at $3.1B+.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered as a prominent Harvard Chan School alumni success story in value-based care transformation.

Strengths

  • Massive scale and impact: $1B+ Medicare savings demonstrates clear value creation.
  • Policy expertise: Founder's government experience provides unique regulatory insight.

Weaknesses

  • Late-stage valuation: $3B+ valuation limits upside for new investors.
  • Medicare policy dependence: Changes in CMS ACO rules could impact business model.

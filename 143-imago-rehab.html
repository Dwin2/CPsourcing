3. Product Overview

Imago Rehab develops a soft robotic glove for at-home stroke rehabilitation. The wearable device uses pneumatic soft actuators to assist hand movements, enabling stroke survivors to perform repetitive therapy exercises at home. The system includes sensors to track progress and gamified exercises to improve patient engagement and compliance.

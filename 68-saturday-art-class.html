68. Saturday Art Class

Visual Arts SEL Program

1. Startup Name

Saturday Art Class

2. Founder Contact

HGSE Team

Harvard Graduate School of Education

President's Innovation Challenge 2024 Finalist

3. Product Overview

Saturday Art Class is a visual arts program developing social-emotional learning (SEL) skills for underserved children in India. The program uses art education as a vehicle to build emotional intelligence, self-expression, and social skills in children who lack access to quality arts education.

4. Funding Stage

Early Stage — President's Innovation Challenge 2024 Finalist.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through Harvard President's Innovation Challenge 2024 finalist announcements.

Strengths

  • Research-backed: SEL through arts has strong evidence base for child development.
  • Scalable in India: Large population of underserved children who could benefit.

Weaknesses

  • Nonprofit model: Arts education for underserved typically requires grant funding.
  • Measurement challenges: SEL outcomes harder to quantify than academic metrics.

82. Sanergy

Urban Sanitation Solutions

1. Startup Name

Sanergy

2. Founder Contact

HKS/HBS Team

Harvard Kennedy School / Harvard Business School

Founded by joint degree students

3. Product Overview

Sanergy provides sanitation solutions and waste-to-value systems for urban slums. The company builds and services toilets in informal settlements, then converts collected waste into valuable products like fertilizer and insect-based animal feed.

4. Funding Stage

Growth Stage — Social enterprise with significant impact investment.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through Harvard SICI social enterprise network and urban development coverage.

Strengths

  • Circular economy: Waste-to-value model creates revenue while solving sanitation.
  • Massive need: 2B+ people lack access to basic sanitation globally.

Weaknesses

  • Complex operations: Managing sanitation logistics in slums is challenging.
  • Market creation: Must build demand for both sanitation services and waste products.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. How many toilets are you currently operating and servicing?
  2. What products do you create from waste and who are the buyers?
  3. How do you ensure service quality and reliability for users?
  4. What is your unit economics — revenue per toilet vs. operating cost?
  5. How are you expanding beyond Nairobi?

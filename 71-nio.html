3. Product Overview

Nio provides interactive adaptive self-study tools helping students ace exams worldwide. The platform uses AI to personalize learning paths, identify knowledge gaps, and deliver targeted practice to help students prepare more effectively for standardized tests and coursework.

4. Funding Stage

Early Stage — Developing through Harvard Innovation Labs accelerator support.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through Harvard Innovation Labs Launch Lab X cohort listings.

Strengths

  • Large market: Global test prep market exceeds $100B annually.
  • AI advantage: Personalized learning can outperform one-size-fits-all approaches.

Weaknesses

  • Competitive market: Khan Academy, Quizlet, and many others compete in adaptive learning.
  • Content development: Building quality exam prep content requires significant investment.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. What exams or subjects does your platform currently cover?
  2. How does your adaptive algorithm personalize learning?
  3. What score improvements have your users achieved?
  4. What is your monetization model — subscription, per-course, or freemium?
  5. How do you compete with established players like Kaplan and Princeton Review?

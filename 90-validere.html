3. Product Overview

Validere developed liquid fingerprinting technology for chemical characterization. The platform uses advanced sensors and AI to characterize oil and gas compositions, enabling energy companies to optimize operations, reduce emissions, and improve trading decisions.

4. Funding Stage

Series B — Raised $80M+ for energy sector expansion.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through Wyss Institute spinout company portfolio and energy technology coverage.

Weaknesses

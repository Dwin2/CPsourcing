59. Amend Surgical

Biocompatible Wound Adhesive

1. Startup Name

Amend Surgical

2. Founder Contact

Wyss Institute Team

Wyss Institute for Biologically Inspired Engineering at Harvard

Developing bio-inspired adhesive technology

3. Product Overview

Amend Surgical develops biocompatible adhesive for sealing wounds after oral surgery. The technology uses bio-inspired adhesive materials that work in wet environments, providing strong tissue bonding to improve healing outcomes and reduce complications from oral surgical procedures.

4. Funding Stage

Early Stage — Developing technology through Wyss Institute support and early funding.

5. How I Found the Company

Discovered through Wyss Institute spinout company portfolio and surgical innovation coverage.

Strengths

  • Clear clinical need: Current oral surgery closure methods have significant limitations.
  • Platform technology: Bio-adhesive could expand to other surgical applications.

Weaknesses

  • Narrow initial market: Oral surgery adhesive is a niche starting point.
  • Surgeon adoption: Surgeons slow to adopt new closure techniques.

7. Five Questions for the Team

  1. What oral surgery procedures is your adhesive designed for?
  2. How does bond strength compare to sutures and existing adhesives?
  3. What is your FDA regulatory pathway — 510(k) or PMA?
  4. How do you plan to expand beyond oral surgery?
  5. What is your manufacturing and supply chain strategy?

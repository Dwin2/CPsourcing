3. Product Overview

MabLab develops 5-in-1 rapid test strips that can detect fentanyl and other dangerous adulterants commonly found in recreational drugs. The strips are designed to be affordable, fast, and accessible to help prevent the over 100,000 annual overdose deaths in the United States. The product uses lateral flow immunoassay technology to provide results in minutes, empowering users to make informed decisions about substance safety.
